Wushu

Wushu is motherland of martial art, is a big concept,is related with all the technic and system of combat and defence, pracitice in a envrioment of deep philosophy, guide by precios human and moral values. with practice we disciplicate or body and find the harmony between the fisical mental. In our practice will learn many chincese cultural traditions and history related with wushu practice. For children and adults As a martial art principe the children will be in correct discipline, rise the teamwork skill, and selfdeveloment.

Taijiquan Qigong

Taijiquan style of wushu, created in the principes of Ying Yang, the coordination and the harmony of the movement is the distintion of this beatiful style. Qigong is practice vital in the life of wushu, execercises with control of our breath we experimet relaxtion and selfmassage, rising up the fisical capacity of our body puting in peace our mind.

Practice for chidren

Wushu is a big plus if start from ealy years, our program include a introductory course for children where thy will learn the basic of practice and concepts of wushu. Starting with to learn 长拳 (north style) what is and offical competion style in the sport events of wushu. Many exercice with hands and legs, combinating flexibity and strength, and coordination speed and control. Practice wushu suit for boys an girls without distintion, not any inicial fisical condition is requiered. for childeren with already expirience in wushu also are a traning program in order to continue their preparation

Practice for young and Adults

Practice wushu is a recomendation in whatever age. The big diversity of wushu style and type of practice make not limitation in age or fisical condition of any type, group practice wushu after lesson or afer work is amazing chance to socializate, and make better your lifestyle. We propose you to start in groups of practice based in Taijiquan and qigong, where the main task is exchange expirience, get knowledges, and have a quality time in our fisic in our mind
